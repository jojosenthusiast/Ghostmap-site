Pricing

Free for devs.
Fair for teams.

GhostMap V1 is free for personal use, students, open source projects and individual developers. Teams and companies fund the road to V2 through transparent, low-cost commercial plans — and early supporters keep a permanent discount on whatever ships next.

Plans

Pick the plan that fits how you use GhostMap.

Every plan ships with the same V1 feature set. The difference is who is allowed to use it commercially, who gets early access to V2, and who locks in a permanent discount as part of the Early Access program.

GhostMap Early Access Program Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026 · Version: 1.0

Purpose of the program

The GhostMap Early Access program was created so that developers, teams and organizations can support the growth and evolution of GhostMap during its early stages.

Participants get early access to new features, participate in early testing and receive special commercial benefits as recognition for their support.

Available plans

GhostMap Community

Free. For individual developers, students, personal projects and open source projects. Includes access to the features available in the Community version.

GhostMap Commercial

Standard commercial license for companies, teams and organizations that use GhostMap for professional or commercial purposes. The price may change as new features, services or capabilities are added to the product.

GhostMap Early Commercial

Early-access commercial license for organizations that want to support the development of GhostMap and access new capabilities ahead of their general release. Includes:

  • Active commercial license.
  • Early access to eligible features.
  • Participation in beta programs.
  • Priority support.
  • Seniority benefits according to the tier reached inside the program.

GhostMap Early Supporter

Program for individual users who want to support GhostMap's development. Includes:

  • Early access to selected features.
  • Participation in beta testing.
  • Exclusive benefits for early members.
  • Seniority benefits according to the tier reached inside the program.

Seniority tier system

Early Access participants receive a seniority tier based on the stage of development at which they joined the program. Tiers reflect the support given during the project's early stages.

Founder Tier

10%
Maximum commercial benefit. Discount on future eligible licenses.

Pioneer Tier

7.5%
Discount on future eligible licenses.

Early Tier

5%
Discount on future eligible licenses.

Supporter Tier

2.5%
Discount on future eligible licenses.

Applying the benefits

Discounts earned through the Early Access tiers:

  • Apply to the then-current price of eligible licenses.
  • Remain in effect while the participant keeps an active subscription in good standing.
  • Are not transferable.
  • Cannot be combined with other promotional programs except with GhostMap's express authorization.

Duration of benefits

Commercial benefits associated with the Early Access program have a maximum duration of five (5) years, counted from the official release date of the first Enterprise version of GhostMap.

After that period, licenses move to the commercial terms in effect at that time. Historical recognition, badges and early-member status may be preserved beyond that period at GhostMap's discretion.

Product evolution

GhostMap may add new features, modules, integrations, collaboration tooling, cloud services or enterprise capabilities. As a result, license prices may change to reflect:

  • New features.
  • Operating costs.
  • Infrastructure costs.
  • Support costs.
  • Research and development costs.
  • Market conditions.

Early Access participants keep the benefits matching their seniority tier in accordance with this policy.

Early access (technical caveat)

Features distributed through early access may:

  • Change.
  • Be modified.
  • Be delayed.
  • Be replaced.
  • Be removed.

GhostMap does not guarantee specific release dates for future features. Roadmaps, demos, proposals and public documents represent development goals only and do not constitute contractual commitments.

Program termination

GhostMap may close the Early Access program at any time. Once closed:

  • No new participants will be admitted.
  • Existing participants keep the tier they earned.
  • Benefits continue to apply in accordance with this policy.

Changes to this policy

GhostMap may update this policy as needed to reflect operational, technical, commercial or legal changes. Changes will not retroactively reduce benefits already earned by active participants, except where required by law or by extraordinary circumstances related to the viability of the service.

About this document. This is a community-drafted, plain-language version of the program policy. It is not a substitute for a written commercial agreement — the final binding terms for any paid plan will be provided as part of the order or license document at sign-up.
