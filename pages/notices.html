Third-Party Notices

GhostMap includes third-party components. Each keeps its own upstream license; the exact upstream LICENSE text ships inside the extension.

Effective date: June 20, 2026 · Version: 1.2

Runtime dependencies

GhostMap's free non-commercial license applies only to original GhostMap code. It does not replace, override, or relicense third-party components, including Tree-sitter, web-tree-sitter, WebAssembly grammars, VS Code, or site fonts. The exact upstream LICENSE text for every bundled component listed below ships inside the extension under wasm/licenses/<component>/, alongside a SOURCE.txt provenance file.

web-tree-sitter · 0.26.8 · MIT. The WebAssembly runtime that powers the Tree-sitter layer of the Ghost Engine. Authored by the tree-sitter project. Source: github.com/tree-sitter/tree-sitter.
